SIKHAY

A student-centered digital platform focused on collaboration,
academic support, and community-driven learning among Honorians.

SIKHAY is a collaborative networking platform built exclusively for Honorians, the students of
Pampanga State University. Our mission is to connect students with their peers to complete
academic or campus-related tasks, share study materials, and collectively own specialized
equipment needed for certain subjects. SIKHAY fosters financial efficiency, accountability, and
community spirit by reducing unnecessary expenses, encouraging cooperation, and
strengthening togetherness among students. Whether it is posting queries, running errands,
tutoring, or sharing reviewers and resources, SIKHAY provides a safe and verified environment
where students can support one another. By requiring PSU email verification, we ensure that
the platform remains authentic and focused on the needs of our academic community. SIKHAY
is more than a website - it is a student-driven initiative that promotes responsibility,
collaboration, and lasting connections across the university.
