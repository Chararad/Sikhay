Privacy Policy

At SIKHAY, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of every Pampanga State University student who joins our platform. We collect only the information necessary to provide our services, including your name, PSU email, profile details, posts, and uploaded resources. This data is used to verify accounts, enable collaboration, and improve the overall user experience. Sikhay does not sell or trade personal information, and any sharing is limited to trusted third‑party providers such as payment processors when transactions occur. We employ industry‑standard safeguards, including encryption, password protection, and moderation systems, to prevent unauthorized access or misuse. Students retain full control over their accounts, with the ability to edit profiles, delete resources, or request account deletion at any time. Cookies are used only to support login sessions and enhance site performance. By using SIKHAY, you consent to the responsible collection and use of your information as described in this policy, which may be updated periodically to reflect new features and improvements. 
Cookies Policy
Sikhay uses cookies and similar technologies to provide a secure, reliable, and personalized experience for Pampanga State University students. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your login sessions, keep you signed in, and improve site performance. We use session cookies to maintain access during your visit and performance cookies to analyze usage trends, optimize features, and enhance collaboration across the platform. Sikhay does not use cookies to collect sensitive personal information, nor do we sell or share cookie data for commercial purposes. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings; however, certain features, such as staying logged in or accessing personalized content, may not function properly without them. By continuing to use Sikhay, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy, which may be updated periodically to reflect new technologies and improvements.
