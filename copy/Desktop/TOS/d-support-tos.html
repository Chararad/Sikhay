Welcome to SIKHAY, a collaborative networking platform designed exclusively for Honorians, the students of Pampanga State University. By accessing or using Sikhay, you agree to comply with these Terms of Service, which outline the rules and responsibilities that protect both our users and the website's integrity. SIKHAY’s mission is to connect students with their peers to complete academic or campus-related tasks, whether in exchange for monetary compensation or simply to build relationships. Through the platform, students may request assistance with errands, tutoring, and other academic tasks, or post queries and concerns for fellow students to answer.

SIKHAY also enables resource sharing, allowing students to upload reviewers and study materials, as well as co-own specialized equipment needed for certain subjects. To ensure fairness and accountability, the system uses pre-made contracts for shared equipment, requiring the responsible party to cover 50% of damages while the remaining 50% is shared among co-owners, with photo or video evidence required before and after use. The student council of the relevant department facilitates access and storage of these shared resources.

All users must register with their official PSU email to reduce scams, maintain appropriate interaction, and safeguard the community. By using SIKHAY, you agree not to upload copyrighted materials without permission, engage in harassment, spam, or misuse of the system, and to respect the collaborative spirit of the platform.

Moderation systems, including reporting and verification, are in place to prevent misuse, and accounts may be suspended or terminated for violations. SIKHAY is provided “as is” without warranties. While we strive to maintain a secure and reliable service, we cannot guarantee uninterrupted access or be held responsible for damages arising from use of the site. By continuing to use SIKHAY, you acknowledge and accept these terms, which may be updated periodically.
